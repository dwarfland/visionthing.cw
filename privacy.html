Privacy Policy

Our apps are committed to preserving your privacy.

Spanish Verbs for Mac

The app does not collect any data without your permission and never communicates with our servers or third parties across the network.

Curaçao Weather App

The app does not collect any data without your permission and, in normal operation, does not transmit any data about you to our servers.

If and only if you choose to submit impacts via the dedicated "Report an Impact" feature, the data you explicitly provide for the report will be transmitted to our servers for further processing by the app and/or the Meteorological Department. Data collected as part of a report includes the (optional) photo and description/comment you provide, and may also include your current GPS location, if you choose to enable that option.

The app uses Sign in with Apple and Google Sign-in (on iOS and Android, respectively) for submitting reports, to avoid spam and abuse. The reports you submit will be internally linked to your account in case the Weather Department needs to follow up with you, but no information about your account will be made available to anybody. Only the type of impact and the rough location will become visible to other users of the app.
